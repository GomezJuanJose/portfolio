Description

Puzzle and trivial game on 2D pixel art in which you play as an explorer who gets lost in a forest and falls into a well, to get out make your way through different levels by moving obstacles and killing enemies in a limited number of moves.
Also at the end of each level you will have to go through a series of questions to complete the level, if you fail you will have to repeat it.

History of the project

It is a university project to learn how the Nintendo DS works internally and better understand the hardware architecture of game consoles, as well as how to learn to program low level games using C and DevKitPro.
It was a two weeks work and I liked the final result.

Features

  • Pass throught five levels in a limited number of movements.
  • Respond correctly a quiz after each level.
  • Animations.
  • Reset level button.
  • Short but challengful game.
