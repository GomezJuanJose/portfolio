Description

A 3d platform and puzzle game with stylized aesthetics where you guide a wolf guardian through a forgotten and beautiful world to help a little puppy.
Enter this small world to face its challenges and dangers to unravel its mysteries.

History of the project

It is a work done for a university subject which proposed to devise, design and develop a game in 3 months.
It was a challenge because not only had to make the game but also to combine it with other subjects of the degree.

Features

  • A single world without loading screens.
  • Physics puzzles.
  • Dangerous enemies that you will have to avoid.
  • Compatible with controller.
  • Translated to english and spanish.
  • Calm gameplay but can be challenging.
