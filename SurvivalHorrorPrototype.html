Description

A prototype of a 3D game which follows the classic formula of Survival Horror but adapted to mobile format.
At the moment it is an incomplete prototype showing the main implemented features of the genre.

History of the project

This prototype was created to be presented as the final work of my professional formation as a multiplatform application developer. I had three months to do it and I had to combine it with the eight hours a day of internship in a company.

Features

  • Fixed cameras which change of angles when pass through specific zones.
  • Inventory.
  • Chest inventory.
  • Life system.
  • Key and locked door system.
  • Auto-aim.
  • AI enemies.
  • Animations state machine.
